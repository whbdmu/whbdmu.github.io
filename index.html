Bios

  • I am an Associate Professor at Liaoning Underwater Robot Engineering Research Center, School of Information Science and Technology of Dalian Maritime University.

  • I received the Ph.D. degree from Dalian University of Technology under the supervision of Prof. Lin Feng in 2018.

  • From Oct. 2016 to Sep. 2017, I was a visiting scholar in Prof. Chunhua Shen's group at UoA, Australia.

  • My research interests focus on computer vision and machine learning, particularly on multi-view learning, object re-id and deep learning models.
